Admission Overview

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs. Admission is strictly based on merit and entrance examination scores. We are committed to providing equal opportunities to all aspiring students.

Programs Offered

B.Tech

4-Year Undergraduate Program in 9 disciplines including CS, AI, ECE, Mech, Civil, and more.

M.Tech

2-Year Postgraduate Program specializing in advanced engineering fields.

MCA

Master of Computer Applications for graduates seeking a career in IT.

PhD

Doctoral programs in various engineering and science disciplines.

Eligibility Criteria

B.Tech

Pass in Higher Secondary Examination with 45% marks in Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry put together.

M.Tech

B.Tech/B.E degree with 60% marks and valid GATE score (preferred).

MCA

BCA/B.Sc/B.Com degree with Mathematics at 10+2 or Graduation level.

Documents Required

  • 10th & 12th Mark Sheets
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Conduct Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if CBSE/ICSE)
  • Entrance Score Card
  • Physical Fitness Certificate
  • 3 Passport Size Photos
